TRUE PROFESSIONALISM.

MOONLIGHT ENTERPRISES is an alliance of independent transportation and logistics companies and key affiliated service providers. These organizations form a synergy to provide an unparalled resource of transportation, value added logistics services and computerized connectivity for customers on a global scale.

AIR FREIGHT

MOONLIGHT ENTERPRISES offers scheduled airfreight services Around the world. From packages and heavyweight cargo we can provide airport‐to‐airport to door‐to‐door service options. Transit times are fast and reliable ‐ ensuring you meet your customer's deadlines.

INLAND LOGISTICS

MOONLIGHT ENTERPRISES provides inland transportation that can deal with any trucking and distribution that you might require. With safety and efficiency on top of the priority list, we ensure that your merchandize arrives safely at the right place.

SEA FREIGHT

MOONLIGHT ENTERPRISES provides worldwide sea freight forwarding and consolidation services for all types of merchandise. Our network of resources enables us to offer a reliable door‐to‐door service for both LCL and FCL shipments.We meet our customer’s service requirements.
